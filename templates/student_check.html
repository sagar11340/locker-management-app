Library & Reading Room Rules

  1. Keep silence in the reading room; mobile phones on silent.
  2. Maintain cleanliness and do not bring food or drinks.
  3. Return books on time. Locker renewals and fines apply.
  4. Report key loss immediately. Key missing fine: ₹150.
  5. Follow instructions of the librarian and staff.
  6. Valid membership ID is required for locker transactions.

LOCKERS – Terms & Conditions

  1. Locker fee: ₹200 per month (non-refundable).
  2. Only ONE key will be issued per locker.
  3. Loss of key attracts a fine of ₹150.
  4. Locker will not be opened if key is forgotten.
  5. No food, weapons, or illegal items allowed.
  6. Locker contents may be checked by the In-Charge.
  7. Sharing lockers is strictly prohibited.
  8. Late fee: ₹10 per day if locker fee is unpaid.
  9. Use lockers at your own risk. The Ashram is not responsible for loss.
